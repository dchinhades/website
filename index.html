    Restaurant

    Several dining options await you at the Terelj Hotel – four restaurants and three lounges – each a special and distinctive experience. Our chef and his team personally select the finest ingredients to create contemporary, internationally inspired fare. The menus for each restaurant change seasonally and our daily specials feature original delicacies. For private dining, our special Room Service menu is available 24 hours a day.We offer the most unique, refined and exquisite dining experience in all of Mongolia, features an original list of cocktails mixed to perfection, over 70 different whiskeys, and a casual yet complete dining menu.

    Resort

    Departure from Terelj Hotel in the morning and visit “Turtle” rock (15min), can climb up the rock through the rough path if dare and enjoy a superb view point over the valley. Then hike to “Aryabal” meditation temple to explore the small Buddhist meditation temple and experience nature photography. Nearby there are plenty of horses and camels for riding. Upon guest choice we may organize Mongolian herding family visit and their living and hospitality experience. After that we transfer to “Chinggis Khaan” Equestrian Monument exploring one of the country’s the first and the tallest equestrian monument of Chinggis Khan.

    Hotel

    Wake up with one of the breakfast menus, return for lunch, and perhaps find yourself spending the afternoon – over views of the Terelj River.Superbly appointed to satisfy any Head of State, the Presidential Suites features a private haute cuisine kitchen, with dining for up to ten beneath a crystal chandelier, complete with tableware by Bernadaud and Fabergé. A graciously proportioned living room comfortably accommodates diplomatic or private functions. The enclosed veranda invites you to play cards, take care of business needs at the antique bureau plat – or watch sunrise over the Mongolian landscape.
