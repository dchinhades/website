Terelj Restaurant

Several dining options await you at the Terelj Hotel – four restaurants and three lounges – each a special and distinctive experience. Our chef and his team personally select the finest ingredients to create contemporary, internationally inspired fare.

Morin Khuur Restaurant

Morin Khuur offers the most unique, refined and exquisite dining experience in all of Mongolia. Enjoy a culinary indulgence of 4 or 5 courses selected from a menu of the chef’s specialties to create your own custom-prepared meal with the finest ingredients. Each course will be paired with the perfect wine, an experience certain to delight. The dining room is centered around a display of traditional Mongolian saddles and artifacts. Two attached private dining rooms are available for private occasions.

Argali

Argali features an original list of cocktails mixed to perfection, over 70 different whiskeys, and a casual yet complete dining menu. Choose from small bite selections, the perfect burger or steak, an international delicacy or a decadent dessert. The comfortable, sophisticated atmosphere service make Argali the perfect place to spend an evening.

River View

Wake up with one of the breakfast menus while watching the sunrise above the Terelj River, the lush, green mountain landscape and blue sky. Return for lunch, and perhaps find yourself spending the afternoon, with menu selections varying from salads, homemade soups, prawns and smoked salmon, to the all-inclusive 3-course plat du jour.

The Café

Get comfortable with a cup of cappuccino and fine French pastry in The Café. Gaze through the sun-filled windows overlooking the Terelj Hotel’s grand entrance, manicured gardens and historic gers, or closely examine the original, contemporary Mongolian artworks on the walls. Each day is a new discovery in culinary creativity with changing selections of smoothies and desserts. The Café is the ideal place for the morning’s first specialty coffee or a well-deserved break after an afternoon of horseback riding.

Lounges

Meet for an aperitif before dinner, sip afternoon tea, or share a fine cigar in one of our three lounges. Our extensive selection of drink lists will be a big plus and we ensure that you will find your favorite drink. Dive deep into the vibrant world below, where you expect the gastronomy, mixology, music, and bubbly moment in the Terelj Restaurant's Lounges

-Lobby Lounge:

Rendezvous with your friends and family or come early for your dinner reservation to sip an aperitif cocktail or champagne in the Lobby Lounge. Plush armchairs offer the ideal place to watch the hustle and bustle of the hotel in comfort and style while enjoying your coffee or tea in the warmth of the afternoon sunlight.

-Tea Lounge:

Afternoon tea in the Tea Lounge is more than a Mongolian tradition. It features a wide selection of more than 35 different flavors from Ronnefeldt Teas. The overstuffed sofas and amber glow of the fireplace create a cozy environment in which to sip and enjoy the company of family and friends.

-Cigar Lounge:

Exchange anecdotes with cigar lovers from around the world in our quiet hideaway, or step onto the balcony to take in the clear mountain air and majesty of Terelj National Park. With rich velvets and comfortable, dark furnishings, the surroundings invite you to relax in elegance and timeless style. Complemented by Argali Bar’s selection of over 70 varieties of whiskey and cognac, the Cigar Lounge offers complete enjoyment for our discriminating guests.
